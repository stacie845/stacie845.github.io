Kirby

Kirby is an 8-inch, pink, spherical creature with short arms, red feet, and big eyes, known for his ability to inhale enemies and copy their powers to gain new abilities

KIRBY'S CREATION

Kirby was orginally made in 1990 by Masahiro Sakurai as a placeholder for Kirby's Dreamland, this creation was intended for bginners as it featured a ball-like design. However, Kirby was organially named Popopo then eventually evolved into what we know now as it was popular with HAL Laboratory Staff
